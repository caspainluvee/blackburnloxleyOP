we are Blackburn Loxley

Whether you are buying a single property or a major portfolio, we are able to provide valuation advice on a wide range of property types, for a variety of purposes

Our advice to our clients is based on extensive and sound knowledge, coupled with an active awareness of the market and a practical approach to problems. Listening to and understanding our clients requirements, we provide a friendly service to assist you in your property matters.
We also undertake statutory valuations which are a product of an act of law that specifies the valuation method which should be used when deciding upon the value required for Inheritance and Capital Gains Tax purposes; Non Domestic Rating Appeals, also Compulsory Purchase and Acquisition.
The fact the valuation is derived through the law should not allow you to become bemused by the process. It is our task to explain to you in plain English how the valuation is derived so feel free to contact us for an initial advice and consultation.
